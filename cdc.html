InfoSphere CDC

There are many ways to connect a Db2 database to IBM® Db2® on Cloud. One way is to capture data that has changed in your on-premises Db2 database with IBM InfoSphere Change Data Capture (InfoSphere CDC), which is a real-time data synchronization solution.

To synchronize your on-premises data in real time to your Db2 on Cloud database with InfoSphere CDC, see this tutorial: How to stay online while moving data to Db2 on Cloud.
