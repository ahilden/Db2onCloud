Communities

There are user-driven communities that you can join for information, tutorials, discussions, and help from professional Db2 users. And it's free to join!

  • International Db2 Users Group (IDUG®) Join thousands of professionals using Db2 with IDUG®, an independent, not-for-profit, user-run organization for Db2 users. The IDUG online forums are a great place to ask for help or get involved. IDUG events around the world let you connect and learn with other Db2 professionals.
  • Db2 on Cloud Community on IBM Communities A Db2 developer community.
  • Stack Overflow A support forum and community for developers.
