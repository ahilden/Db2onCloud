Maintenance and notification policies

The maintenance and notification policies for the IBM® Db2® on Cloud service can be found in the IBM Cloud service descriptions . Additional terms of service specifically for Db2 on Cloud can be found by looking under the Chargeable additional SDs category.

Being acquainted with our policies can better help you plan and schedule your workloads to be resilient to system maintenance.

Maintenance policy

Effective as of March 27, 2018, in the event that scheduled system maintenance must be done for the Db2 on Cloud service, the major data centers (London, Dallas, and Sydney) are updated on separate days of the business week. All of the other data centers are simultaneously updated on a day of the week that doesn't coincide with the major data centers. Table 1 shows an example of a Db2 on Cloud system maintenance schedule.

Table 1. Example of a system maintenance schedule
Data center Example of a possible maintenance schedule
London Monday
Dallas Tuesday
Sydney Wednesday
All others Thursday

The table is not an official system maintenance schedule, but an example of a possible maintenance schedule that demonstrates how maintenance might be done on different days for a given update.

Notification policy

Effective as of March 27, 2018, notification is sent to you two business days in advance of any scheduled system maintenance that can impact your Db2 on Cloud service.

The Db2 on Cloud notification policy excludes the case in which we must address, in a timely manner, any security vulnerability that we deem to be an emergency-grade, time-sensitive issue. In that case, at least 24 hours advance notice is given as stipulated in the IBM Cloud service descriptions.
